How to prepare your video to allow Progress tracking via Workflow?

1. A video needs to be encoded for it to show up in the filters for a Video Tracking Workflow Trigger.
2. We recommend turning off Show Progress Bar in the Video Element settings so viewers cannot move back and forwards in a video, to assure accurate progress tracking.

3. You are also recommended to set up a Redirect for Unregistered Visitors.
