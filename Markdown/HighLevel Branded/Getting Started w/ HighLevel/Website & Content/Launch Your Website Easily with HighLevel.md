## **Launching a Website on Your Domain**

  
Ready to get started? Great!

1. **Navigate to Sites > Websites**: First, let's head to the Websites section to kick things off.
2. **Click New Website**: Look for the "New Website" button. Easy to find, right?
3. **Choose Your Starting Point**:  
   * **From Blank**: Feeling creative and adventurous? This gives you total freedom!  
   * **From Templates**: Want something polished and quick? Let's start here.
4. **Select From Templates and Click Create**: Browse through our gorgeous templates. Once you've found your match, click "preview" to see it live, then hit **Continue**.  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047027664/original/3BMWKGNMWDsAPNPNWVSRxMPxFgHrA6_lYw.png?1747858808)

Next, you'll customize your site:

1. **In Website Editor, Configure Website**: Play around, tweak colors, add images, and really make it yours.
2. **Click Save**: Don't forget to save your masterpiece!
3. **Click Publish**: Make it live for the whole world.
4. **Click Back (to exit Website Editor)**: Time to wrap things up and get your site online.  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047027728/original/lcJ99sRNqVanMhlRKb94entAWtmhsnUfxg.png?1747859001)

Let's make it official by connecting your domain:

1. **Click Settings Tab**: Jump over to the settings to connect your unique web address.
2. **Under Domain Click Connect Domain**: Find the domain dropdown and click the **Connect Domain** link underneath.  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047694751/original/5MsqqMucysJiz346jo-Kz3DaRw8pKBEp7Q.png?1748985779)  
After clicking Connect Domain you might go straight to this page, in which cast you can enter your domain and click **Continue**, or you can click **Back to Domains** to go to the Settings > Domains list of domains.  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047694725/original/HIZcSBbPGy3Qo8ca7G-er6-KrDTMbFubbg.png?1748985699)
3. **Click Connect a Domain**: Enter your domain name, and click "Continue" twice. Almost there!  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047027780/original/1And6P2wSO3emhikoJuwmhw9V1EBQcZSfA.png?1747859130)  
    
If you don't have any domains yet, this is the page you will see. Just click **Connect a Domain**.  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047694670/original/AHXjBPAsM5OWi3ft5Za1O48bUycV4bbsfQ.jpeg?1748985597)
4. **Click to connect a website:** Use the option for blog/funnel/website/etc.  
    
**![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047027805/original/BEOO6wwonOXBcApMRm1pP6ByvhdWyM64PA.png?1747859217)**
5. **Enter the domain and click Continue:** This is where you specify the domain you want your website to launch on.  
    
**![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047027850/original/pC01vugqGJpcbxDVPHJz6BpG6joMpUWTnw.png?1747859297)**
6. **Continue and Configure DNS (varies by registrar)**: Follow the provided instructions. Don't worry, it's simpler than it sounds!  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047027884/original/-2PNL8qpJhKF2wCgtsHhpNmV39IbwSnYyg.png?1747859367)
7. **Click Verify Records**: Let's make sure everything checks out.  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047027903/original/aDQbcdUwFe7XCyb-zaftZQcLOkVxRi-3Hw.png?1747859446)
8. **Navigate back to Website Settings Tab**: Return to settings one last time.  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047027911/original/hme70Pp7-ch7604JItcWwVtY1ChEwrSAFQ.png?1747859497)
9. **Select Domain and Click Save**: Choose your shiny new domain and save your changes. Congratulations, your website is live!  
    
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155047027923/original/icLXWwV6Zjk1PMOKGSW6uAFx7XRqf4eSQA.png?1747859532)

**Next,** you can share your new website link with your audience, start exploring analytics, or add more content to enhance your online presence. Happy launching!
